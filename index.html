About

Hi! I’m Bill Sun. I’m a first-year undergraduate student at Columbia University interested in pursuing a career at the crossroads of computer science, biomedical engineering, and healthcare. Currently, my main research interests involve the application of machine learning and deep learning techniques to improve human healthcare. I strive to integrate my knowledge of web development with my understanding of machine learning to create easily-accessible and accurate diagnostic processes to assist patients afflicted with various diseases.
